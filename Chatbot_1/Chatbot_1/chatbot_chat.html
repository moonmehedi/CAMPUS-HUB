Students at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) can retake a failed course in the following ways: 
Supplementary or self-study examination: Students can take a supplementary or self-study examination to pass a failed course. The maximum grade for a supplementary or self-study examination is B+.
Regular or short term: Students can retake a failed course in a regular or short term.
Students can only retake a failed course twice, but with the permission of the Academic Council of MIST, they may be allowed to retake it a third time. 
If a student fails a course, it will be considered a backlog subject. Students must pass the backlog subject in the next scheduled re-examination. If they don't, they may be withdrawn from the institution. 
When a course is repeated for improvement, the better grade is counted for the calculation of GPA.
